ATMOSPHERIC TROPICAL VILLA

In Curaçao they call Villa Bajano a tropical home. Beautiful Spanish marble tiles run throughout the entire house. Even extending up the walls in the bathrooms. All windows in the house have shutter windows (with insect screens) so the wind naturally cools the house. There are three air-conditioned bedrooms in the house, 2 bathrooms and a separate toilet. The wonderful tropical sunny atmosphere is enhanced by tasteful Ibiza accessories inside and outside.

Floor plan

At the front of the house the living room, dining area and open kitchen are connected. The living room has large sliding doors. You can also walk straight onto the terrace through the hallway. In the hallway there is a toilet and a utility room with, among other things, a washer-dryer combination.

At the rear are three bedrooms and two bathrooms. The master bedroom has its own bathroom including toilet. The other bedrooms share the second bathroom. This also has a separate toilet. All rooms have a large wardrobe.

Living room

From the living room you have a lovely view of the pool and terrace. There is a large corner sofa and also a separate seating area on the other side of the room. The shutter windows provide a cool breeze. From the corner sofa you watch a series, film or sporting event on the 55 inch smart TV.

Kitchen and dining area

The villa has a well-equipped modern kitchen with dishwasher, combi oven-microwave, Nespresso machine, fridge-freezer and extensive inventory. Plenty of options to prepare a drink or cook up something delicious.

From the handleless kitchen you look onto the dining area

Dining table and bar stools at the kitchen counter

Bedrooms and bathrooms

The villa has three bedrooms all with comfortable box spring beds. The master bedroom and 2nd bedroom have a double box spring bed. The 3rd bedroom has two separate single box spring beds. All bedrooms have their own large wardrobe. The master bathroom has a large walk-in shower, double sink and private toilet. The second bathroom also has a walk-in shower, sink and a separate toilet. All bedrooms and bathrooms have shutter windows that provide fresh air and cool.
