More about the location

Why do guests choose Villa Bajano?

Villa Bajano has been a favourite place for our guests from the very first moment. The combination of the prime location, the spacious private pool and the quiet, sheltered garden makes it the ideal place to fully unwind. With room for up to six people and a warm, relaxed atmosphere, it immediately feels comfortable and informal.

The villa stands out for its character: not a stark, clinical design style, but an atmospheric interior with marble tiles and an elegant combination of Ibiza style and Caribbean influences.

We also offer excellent value for money, something our guests confirm time and again. We look forward to welcoming you soon!

Tastefully furnished home

Two years ago the villa was renovated and refurnished. A modern white handleless kitchen was installed and the house is tastefully decorated with many Ibiza and Curaçao accessories.

Guests feel at home

The house is bright and feels comfortable. For up to 6 people it is nicely spacious but not too large. It has all amenities and the shutter windows always let in a lovely breeze.

Location, location, location

Villa Bajano is perfectly located next to Jan Thiel Beach in Curaçao. The villa is a 2-minute walk from a supermarket and a 5-minute walk from the beach. The road it is on is quiet in terms of traffic.

Garden with great privacy

Due to the elevated position from the street and the beautiful tropical vegetation, nobody can look into your garden. You can enjoy the pool, the sun loungers and the covered terrace in complete peace.

Villa Bajano impressions

Pool and terrace

From the sliding doors of the living room you step straight into the pool. There are sun loungers to relax in the sun. For shade, head to the covered terrace. Here you'll find a dining area and a lounge sofa and chair. Cook outside on the Weber gas barbecue.

Kitchen and living room

The house has a new white handleless kitchen with all the comfort you'd expect. Behind the kitchen is a dining area for indoor dining. And in the living room there is a 55″ LG smart TV with all streaming services. Of course there is fast internet throughout the villa.

Bedrooms and bathrooms

The villa has 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms. The master bedroom has its own bathroom. The second bathroom is shared by the other 2 bedrooms. All bedrooms have comfortable box spring beds and of course air conditioning.
