BEAUTIFUL GARDEN

When you open the sliding doors of the living room you can walk straight into the pool. On one side of the pool are sun loungers. On the other side is a covered terrace with both a dining table and a sofa. Here you have shade and the terrace is well positioned in the wind. Throughout the garden you enjoy complete privacy. Many tropical flowers and plants grow here. Beautiful trees stand in the garden behind the house. On the slope from the street to the house there is enough space to park several cars.

Plot overview

The plot is 900 m2. The house is about 2.5 metres above street level. This gives you ultimate privacy. The front garden is over 200 m2. The large pool is 9m by 5m. The dimensions of the villa are 13 by 13 metres. Behind it is also a back garden.

Swimming pool

A spacious pool where you want to spend all day relaxing or doing laps. In Curaçao the water temperature is always lovely.

Covered terrace

On the terrace there is a dining area, a lounge sofa and a lounge chair. For many guests it is the favourite spot in the villa.

Outdoor kitchen

Why go inside to cook when you can do it outside? On the terrace there is a Weber Genesis barbecue, the ideal outdoor kitchen and suitable for serious grilling.

Driveway

The driveway has space for several cars and is secured with an electric gate for privacy and security.
